# Rust Raid Prep Pack Outline

This is the first paid-product outline for manual delivery after a real payment link is configured.

## Pack Contents

1. Raid target scoring worksheet.
2. Solo and duo pre-raid checklist.
3. Sulfur target planning sheet.
4. Upkeep weekly planning sheet.
5. Post-raid review checklist.

## Manual Fulfillment Flow

1. Confirm PayPal payment status is paid.
2. Record the transaction in `delivery-log-template.csv`.
3. Send the pack to the buyer email.
4. Record delivery timestamp and method.
5. Save any buyer support/refund notes in the order row.

## Do Not Ship Until

- PayPal Business account is confirmed.
- Real payment link is added to `config.js`.
- Product file is finalized.
- Delivery record process is tested with a sandbox or personal test purchase.
