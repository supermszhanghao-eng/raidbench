Palworld beginner progression checklist

A practical Palworld beginner progression checklist for early survival, base setup, Pal capture, crafting, and boss readiness.

View planning credits Back to Palworld Lab

Short answer

A good Palworld start balances survival, base automation, capture coverage, and boss prep. Do not rush only combat or only base building; the fastest progress comes from a base that supplies your next capture and upgrade loop.

StageMain goalAvoid
First baseFood, beds, storage, basic production.Building too far from resources before you can move materials.
Capture loopGet Pals for work suitability and combat coverage.Keeping only high-combat Pals and ignoring base workers.
CraftingUnlock tools, weapons, armor, and transport upgrades.Spending resources on comfort before survival.
Boss prepBring damage, healing, and type coverage.Entering fights with random Pals and no plan.

Checklist

  • Secure food and basic storage first.
  • Capture workers for gathering, handiwork, kindling, watering, and mining.
  • Upgrade transport before expanding too far.
  • Keep one combat team and one base team in mind.
  • Prepare for bosses with type coverage and recovery items.

Example

If your base cannot produce materials for spheres, ammo, or repairs, your exploration loop slows down even if your combat team is strong.

Common mistakes

  • Moving base too early without resource planning.
  • Ignoring worker suitability.
  • Fighting bosses before the team has a clear role split.
  • Using every rare Pal in combat when one should be working at base.
