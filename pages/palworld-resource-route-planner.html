Palworld resource route planner

A Palworld resource route planner for deciding what to farm, where the bottleneck is, and when to move or automate production.

View planning credits Back to Palworld Lab

Short answer

The next resource should be the bottleneck blocking your next unlock, boss attempt, base upgrade, or capture loop. Farming everything equally feels productive but often delays the one upgrade that matters.

GoalLikely bottleneckDecision
Boss attemptGear, ammo, recovery, team upgrades.Farm only what supports the attempt.
Base upgradeConstruction and station inputs.Check automation before manual farming.
Capture loopSphere materials and travel support.Farm inputs for repeated captures.
BreedingFood, space, and parent tracking.Stabilize base support first.

Checklist

  • Name the goal before farming.
  • Write the one missing material that blocks it.
  • Check whether base automation can solve it.
  • Plan a route that ends near storage or fast travel.
  • Re-score routes after map or resource updates.

Example

If your next goal is a boss attempt, farming building materials may be less useful than gathering the inputs for weapons, armor, ammo, or team upgrades.

Common mistakes

  • Farming whatever is nearby without a target.
  • Ignoring fast travel and storage distance.
  • Manually farming a material your base could automate.
  • Expanding before solving the current bottleneck.
