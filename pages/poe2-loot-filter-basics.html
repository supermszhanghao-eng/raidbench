POE2 loot filter basics

Keep early drops readable while avoiding fragile price claims that can change with the economy.

What to filter for first

  • Useful weapon bases for your current skill plan.
  • Linked or socket-relevant items during leveling.
  • Currency and crafting items that matter across builds.
  • High-impact rares worth checking, not every rare on the floor.
