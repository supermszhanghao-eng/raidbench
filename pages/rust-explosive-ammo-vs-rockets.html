Rust explosive ammo vs rockets

Explosive ammo often wins on raw sulfur for single targets. Rockets win when speed, splash, or pressure matters.

Simple rule

If you only need one door and can shoot without being interrupted, explosive ammo is attractive. If you need to break several pieces quickly or use splash, rockets usually become easier to justify.
