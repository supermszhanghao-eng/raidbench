Rust solo raid guide

Solo raids need tighter planning because every delay gives counters more time. Use sulfur estimates to choose targets you can finish and leave.

Estimate solo raid cost

Solo raid priorities

  • Prefer smaller bases with clear door paths.
  • Bring enough boom for one extra layer, not just the visible layer.
  • Choose C4 or rockets when speed matters more than sulfur efficiency.
  • Use explosive ammo only when you can shoot safely.

Simple solo estimate

For a small two-door target, budget at least two sheet metal doors plus a buffer. That might mean 8 satchels, 2 C4, 4 rockets, or about 126 explosive ammo before loot doors and surprises.
