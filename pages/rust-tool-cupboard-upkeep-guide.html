Rust tool cupboard upkeep guide

Tool cupboard upkeep is the base maintenance number that decides whether your base stays alive while you are offline.

Calculate weekly upkeep

What to check

  • Daily wood, stone, metal, and HQM upkeep after each expansion.
  • How many hours or days remain in the tool cupboard.
  • Whether one material category is much lower than the rest.
  • Whether new doors or honeycomb changed the weekly target.

Weekly planning

Multiply daily upkeep by seven, then keep a buffer before a long roam, raid, or offline window. The in-page estimator is built for this quick conversion.
