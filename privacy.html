Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026. This policy explains what RaidBench collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

RaidBench can collect basic usage information such as page views, calculator interactions, guide clicks, browser type, approximate region, and referrer data. If you submit an early-access or interest form, we may collect the email address and player type you provide.

Do not submit sensitive personal information through the site.

How we use information

  • Measure whether calculators, guides, and paid offers are useful.
  • Improve content quality and decide which game guides to maintain.
  • Respond to support, refund, or delivery requests if payments are enabled.
  • Detect abuse, spam, or technical issues.

Analytics and cookies

RaidBench is designed to support privacy-conscious analytics. Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies after a measurement provider is configured. You can limit cookies through your browser settings.

Payments

When paid products are enabled, checkout may be handled by a third-party payment provider such as PayPal, Stripe, Paddle, Lemon Squeezy, or Creem. Payment providers process payment details under their own privacy policies. RaidBench should not store raw card numbers.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers used for hosting, analytics, payments, email delivery, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.

Data retention

Interest-form data and order records should be kept only as long as needed for product validation, support, accounting, dispute handling, and legal obligations.

Children

RaidBench is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child submitted personal information, contact us so it can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact: support@raidbench.com.

RaidBench is an unofficial fan-made planning tool and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Facepunch Studios.
